Break-Even Load Factor Analyzer

Enter flight economics parameters. The tool instantly computes break-even load factor (BELF), RASM vs CASM gap, profit per flight and sensitivity. Useful for quick scenario checks in pricing, capacity planning and network development.

Ready.
CASM is entered in US cents per available seat mile; tool converts to cost per seat for the stage length. Taxes reduce net yield for break-even calculation.

Break-Even Load Factor

—

RASM (Projected)

—
Passenger + ancillary revenue / ASM

CASM (Input)

—
Operating cost / ASM

Profit per Flight

—

Revenue / Pax (Net)

—
Base + ancillary − taxes

Seat Cost (Stage)

—
Variable portion (CASM × stage)

Methodology

BELF = (Fixed Cost + Variable Cost per Seat × Seats) / (Net Revenue per Pax × Seats). Variable cost per seat is CASM (in USD) × stage length in miles (CASM¢ /100 × miles). Net revenue per pax subtracts taxes (if they do not contribute to covering operating cost). Profit per flight uses projected load factor.
