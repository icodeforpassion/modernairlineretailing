ATPCO Fare Filing Explained

ATPCO fare filing is the process airlines use to publish fares, rules, and related pricing data used across distribution channels.

Airlines use fare filing to manage price points, combinability, advance purchase, refunds, and sales conditions.

Fare rules and categories structure these controls. Filed content feeds GDS shopping and can coexist with NDC and offer management strategies.
